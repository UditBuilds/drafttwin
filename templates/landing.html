How It Works

From chaos to control in 3 steps

No training models, no prompt engineering, no integrations. Just you, your brand, and a twin that actually gets it.

Fill a simple 10-minute onboarding form — products, prices, policies, tone of voice, and the things you'd never say. We turn it into your AI brain file: a living document your twin reads before every single reply.
